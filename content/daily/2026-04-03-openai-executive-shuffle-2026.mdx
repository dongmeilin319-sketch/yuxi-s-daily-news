<AIAbstract>OpenAI confirmed executive changes: COO Brad Lightcap moves to lead "special projects" (complex deals and investments) reporting to CEO Sam Altman; Chief Revenue Officer Denise Dresser temporarily assumes some COO duties. AGI development CEO Fidji Simo takes medical leave for several weeks for a neuroimmune condition, with president Greg Brockman managing product during her absence. CMO Kate Rouch steps down to focus on cancer recovery, to return in a "different, more narrowly scoped role" later; company plans to search for a new CMO. OpenAI states leadership remains focused on frontier research and serving nearly 1 billion global users.</AIAbstract>

<Insight title="短期">Denise Dresser将临时承担Brad Lightcap的部分COO职责，Greg Brockman将在Fidji Simo病假期间管理产品，确保近10亿用户服务及企业业务的连续性。公司将启动新CMO的搜寻工作以填补Kate Rouch的空缺。</Insight>
<Insight title="中期">Brad Lightcap转任特别项目后，将主导公司复杂的交易与投资事务，可能涉及重大战略合作或并购活动。Fidji Simo与Kate Rouch康复后的角色调整，将影响OpenAI的AGI开发路线图及市场营销策略。</Insight>
